About

CHRIST (Deemed-to-be University) Online

CHRIST (Deemed to be University), was established in 1969, and is renowned for its innovative curriculum, holistic education, and global academic standards. Being accredited with an A+ grade by NAAC and recognised as an Institution with Potential for Excellence by the UGC, CHRIST offers a variety of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programmes. Boasting of multiple campuses in Bengaluru, Pune Lavasa, and Delhi NCR, the university promotes and fosters a diverse and inclusive environment, attracting students from across India and numerous foreign countries. Committed to excellence in education, research, and cultural activities, CHRIST continues to empower students to achieve their academic and professional goals.

Programmes Offered

Christ University Online offers a diverse range of online degree courses especially curated to provide flexible and accessible education for learners across the globe. These programmes combine rigorous academic standards with practical industry knowledge, ensuring students gain skills that are relevant and career-oriented. With expert faculty guidance and interactive platforms, students experience a supportive learning environment of online higher education. The curriculum is aligned with contemporary trends and emphasizes real-world application, preparing graduates for success in their chosen fields.

B.Com

The Programme is designed to build strong foundation skills required in administering Business Enterprises and its Core functions such as Accounting, Finance, Taxation, Marketing and Human Resource.

Duration: 2 Years

BCA

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is an undergraduate programme that focuses on building software professionals with strong practical and theoretical knowledge in Computer Science.

Duration: 2 Years

MA (Applied Economics)

The postgraduate course in Applied Economics offers a judicious blend of economic theory accounting for interdisciplinarity and empirical measurement tools with intensive specialisations in areas of finance and development.

Duration: 2 Years

M.Sc (AI & ML)

The 2 year programme comprises foundational courses from computer science and advanced courses in the area of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, including Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning, Image processing, Soft Computing and IIoT.

Duration: 2 Years

M.Sc (Data Science)

The Master of Data Science (MDS) is a postgraduate program that combines computer science and statistics to foster proficiency in data-driven decision-making. The curriculum blends theory of data science with hands-on experience in relevant domains and tools.

Duration: 2 Years

MA (English with Communication Studies)

The Master of Arts (English with Communication Studies) programme, established to foster a deep and dynamic engagement with literary and cultural studies, aspires to instill a robust academic interest in both traditional and contemporary literary theories.

Duration: 2 Years

MCA

This postgraduate programme is designed to bridge the gap between computer science theory and real-world application by covering core areas like Data Structures, Algorithms, DBMS, Operating Systems, Web Technologies and Cloud Computing,

Duration: 2 Years
